What's it about?

We unearth and question the assumptions underlying the ways we influence each other, our cities, and nature, and the ways they influence us.

We'll explore:

  • Intersectional justice
  • Wise urbanism
  • Restoration ecology

We began developing the first season in March 2015, and expect to start releasing episodes in May 2015. Stay in touch through Twitter, and join our mailing list for updates.
